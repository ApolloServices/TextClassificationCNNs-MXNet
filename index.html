Text Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks

A Convolutional Neural Network has been trained on 1.4M Amazon reviews, belonging to 7 categories, to predict what the category of a product is based solely on its reviews. You can try it live below, type a review for an hypothetical product and check the results!
